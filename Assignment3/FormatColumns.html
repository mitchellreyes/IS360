New Horizon Series

Transform your home with the versatile and elegant designs of the New Horizon Patio Door Series.

Available in a variety of configurations including 2-panel, 3-panel and 4-panel, and optional overhead transoms, this series will complement any home while offering you unparalleled choices.


Product Features

  • Heavy duty plated steel tandem rollers glide above debris on a raised stainless steel monorail track system for continued smooth and easy operation.
  • Structural metal reinforcement within vertical sash rails enhance performance against outside elements
  • Fusion welded frame and sash construction insures structural integrity with long-term beauty and durability.
  • Triple seal positive interlocking at meeting rail provides weather tightness and forced entry resistance.
  • Optional hardware choices.
